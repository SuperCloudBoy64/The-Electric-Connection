Welcome To The Electric Connection

Your Experienced Columbus Electrical Contractors

For over 40 years, homeowners and businesses have trusted us for dependable electrical services throughout Columbus and Westerville, Ohio. The Electric Connection has proudly served Central Ohio since 1983 as a locally owned and operated electrical contractor built on honesty, quality workmanship, and long-lasting customer relationships.

Our licensed electricians provide a full range of residential and commercial electrical services, including electrical repairs, troubleshooting, panel upgrades, lighting installations, surge protection, and new construction work. Every project is completed with safety, code compliance, and attention to detail as our top priorities.

We believe great electrical service goes beyond wires and panels — it’s about clear communication, showing up on time, respecting your home or business, and standing behind our work. That commitment has kept us serving the Columbus and Westerville communities with pride for more than four decades.
